SISSA Algebraic Geometry Seminar 2022–2023

This is the webpage of the SISSA Algebraic Geometry Seminar. We meet every Monday at 16:00 in Room 136. Talks are 90 minutes long. The tentative topic is

Derived categories and Bridgeland stability conditions.

We will start with an introduction to triangulated categories and derived functors following D. Huybrechts' book Fourier–Mukai transforms in Algebraic Geometry, then we will proceed towards Bridgeland stability conditions following (probably a subset of) the following references:
  • T. Bridgeland, Stability conditions on triangulated categories, Annals of Mathematics, 166 (2007), 317–345.
  • D. Huybrechts, Introduction to stability conditions.
  • E. Macr√¨, Stability conditions on curves, Math. Res. Lett. 14 (2007), no. 4, 657–672.
  • A. Bayer, A tour to stability conditions on derived categories.
