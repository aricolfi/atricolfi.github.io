  • Friday, May 21th at 10:30, Enrico Fatighenti (IMT Toulouse).

    Title: Fano varieties from homogeneous vector bundles

    Abstract: The idea of classifying Fano varieties using homogeneous vector bundles was behind Mukai's classification of prime Fano 3-folds. In this talk, we give a survey of some recent progress along the same lines, including a biregular rework of the non-prime Mori-Mukai 3-folds classification and some examples of higher-dimensional Fano varieties with special Hodge-theoretical properties.
